DAX Measures
=============
Total Sales =
SUMX(
    Sales,
    RELATED(Products[Price]) * Sales[Quantity]
)

Total Orders =
DISTINCTCOUNT(Sales[OrderID])

Total Customers =
DISTINCTCOUNT(Customers[CustomerID])

Total Quantity =
SUM(Sales[Quantity])

Profit =
[Total Sales] * 0.18
